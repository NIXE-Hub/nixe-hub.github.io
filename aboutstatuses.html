About NIXE Hub Statuses

Statuses are tags attributed to a NIXE Hub member that can only be altered by an admin or an owner. Many of the statuses are designed to bring trust to the community, and to stop impersonators.

Many of the statuses are listed below:

  • Member: The default status given to members when they first sign up.
  • Trusted Member: The status given to members who are very active in the community.
  • YouTuber: The status given to members who have good quality content on their YouTube channel.
  • Collaborator: The status given to members who help on the GitHub repository for NIXE Hub.
  • Moderator: The status given to the Forum and LiftLogger moderators.
  • Admin: The status given to admins who keep NIXE Hub running.
  • Owner: The status given to the NIXE Hub owner, NIXE.
