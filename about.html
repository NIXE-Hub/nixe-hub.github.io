About NIXE Hub

Welcome to NIXE Hub, a hub for all things lift (or elevator in the US) related! Made by NIXE (or James, as he's called), this hub will serve anyone who has a passion for lifts. Join now if you haven't already, we need you to be a good community member!

About NIXE himself

NIXE (or James) has always loved lifts since a very early age, and had created his Scratch account in January 2018.

NIXE is now working on NIXE Hub to bring you the best features! If you would like to contact him about anything, please message on his Scratch profile, tell him on GitHub, or just send him an e-mail!
